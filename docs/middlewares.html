Middlewares

OrganicJS suggests you to define middlewares at two levels: application level and router level.

Application Level Middlewares

All your application level middlewares must be defined via app.use in the middlewares/main.js file. This is the file that the framework loads while booting your application server. For ease of maintainence, you may define your middlewares in the middlewares/definition folder and refer to them in the middlewares/main.js file.
